Dear Mrs Sample

I wanted to thank you personally for giving children the childhood they deserve.

We can only protect children from abuse because of people like you.

By supporting us, you’ve let a child somewhere know that they’re not alone. That someone believes in them. And that someone is you – thank you.

I can promise you that every donation you make will be spent carefully, where it can make the biggest difference to a child who needs it.

And over the next few months, we’ll let you know exactly how your support is making a direct difference to children’s lives. I believe it is the least we can do.

I hear shocking stories of cruelty inflicted on children. But at least once a day I’m also reminded of the inspiring lengths people go to protect these children. And at the centre of it all are people like you, who without fuss or fanfare give us the support that makes it all happen.

You’re there for children who need you, and I can’t thank you enough for that.

Yours sincerely

Peter Wanless
Chief Executive

PS Sometimes the scale of abuse can seem overwhelming – but please rest assured your support gets us closer to ending it.
