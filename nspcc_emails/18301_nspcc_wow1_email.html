Dear Mrs Sample

It’s because of your support that I can be there for children like Rebecca.

When I met Rebecca she was hurt and angry.

She’d been abused and neglected since she was a baby. She couldn’t put her pain into words so I encouraged her to draw instead.

At first Rebecca’s pictures showed her hurt and anger. But gradually, as she started to trust me, her paintings changed.

This is the picture she drew for me after a year.

Because of your support, children like Rebecca can look forward to a brighter future.

Thank you.

Sarah, Play Therapist
NSPCC

PS. Your support means we can help traumatised children express themselves and begin to move on through art therapy sessions.
